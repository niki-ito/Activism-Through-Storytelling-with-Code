About me, you, & us

Listen to conversations between people from the Asian community to learn about ME (the speaker), YOU (the listener), and US (the community).
This project aims to highlight narratives of the Asian community that are intimate and less represented in mainstream media. With this, I hope to cultivate further understanding and compassion for the Asian community in the United States.
