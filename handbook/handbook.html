a step-by-step guide

This handbook introduces a step-by-step example of Activism Through Storytelling with Code. The modeled project is a code-based visual narrative website titled “about Me, You, & Us.” This example will act as a utility for artists, designers, activists, and coders on learning how to narrate and visualize stories to advocate for important topics using p5.js..
